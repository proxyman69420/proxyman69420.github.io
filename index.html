Keybinds for Chromebook

Here's an easy-to-use list of keybinds on Chromebook!
CTRL+C - Copy

CTRL+V - Paste

CTRL+X - Cut

CTRL+H - Open history

CTRL+W - Close current tab

CTRL+SHIFT+W - Close all tabs

CTRL+N - Open new window

CTRL+SHIFT+N - Open new window in incognito mode

CTRL+F - Find on page

CTRL+1 to 8 - Jump to tab

CTRL+9 - Jump to rightmost tab

CTRL+TAB - Jump to next tab

CTRL+SHIFT+N - Open whatever in in the search bar in a new window
I will be adding more to this list as I find them out! In the meantime, you can check the official list from google here.
